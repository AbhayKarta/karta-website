About Us

At Karta Consultancy, we specialize in providing tailored financial and strategic advisory services to companies across various sectors and geographies. Our expertise encompasses fund raising, treasury management, corporate advisory including virtual CFO services, all aimed at fostering growth and success for our clients.

Our comprehensive suite of services includes:

  • Debt Syndication: Project finance, structured transactions, working capital debt and restructuring.
  • Investment Banking: Debt capital markets and distressed asset advisory.
  • Fund Raising: For NBFCs for onward lending, real estate, and infrastructure loans.
  • Trade Finance: Offering innovative solutions to meet diverse financing needs.
  • Real Estate Debt Funding: Providing specialized advisory and funding for real estate projects.

With an extensive network of lenders including banks, NBFCs, mutual funds, private wealth aggregators, insurance companies, and development finance institutions, we leverage our deep understanding of the lending landscape to secure optimal financing solutions.

At Karta Consultancy, we are committed to delivering customized financial strategies and advisory services that drive long-term growth.
